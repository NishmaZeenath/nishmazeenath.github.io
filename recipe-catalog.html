Our special Indian recipies.


Mains


1.Butter Chicken

  • Butter Chicken, also known as "Murgh Makhani," is a popular and flavorful dish in Indian cuisine. Originating from the Indian subcontinent, this dish is characterized by tender chicken pieces cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based curry.


2.Kashmiri Lamb Chops

  • Kashmiri Lamb Chops are a delectable culinary delight from the Kashmir region in India. This dish features succulent lamb chops marinated in a flavorful blend of Kashmiri spices, which often include saffron, fennel seeds, ginger, and aromatic herbs.


Appetizer


1. Chile-Cilantro Naan

  • Chile-Cilantro Naan is a delightful variation of the classic Indian flatbread, naan. This version is infused with the vibrant flavors of fresh green chilies and cilantro. The naan is skillfully crafted, incorporating finely chopped green chilies and fragrant cilantro into the dough, resulting in a bread that is both aromatic and slightly spicy.

Dessert


1. Saffron and almond Kulfi

  • Saffron and Almond Kulfi is a luscious and traditional frozen dessert originating from Indian cuisine. Infused with the rich essence of saffron, known for its distinct floral notes, and complemented by the nutty goodness of almonds, this kulfi offers a delightful sensory experience.
